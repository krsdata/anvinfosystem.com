Peter Bronsveld – UK

Peter Bronsveld was born in Brazil, moved to The Netherlands when he was a teenager and currently lives with his family in the United Kingdom. He started his business in 1992 and has been active in the field of fundraising, public relations and communications since.

Peter is a board member of the Dutch branch of the human rights organisation Jubilee Campaign, founded by Lord Alton in the UK in 1987. The foundation has a consultant status at the United Nations, which allows the submission of reports directly to the General Assembly and the International Criminal Court in The Hague.

Peter’s affinity with Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) aspects in business is a result of his involvement with humanitarian aid, human rights and development projects around the world. He is often engaged as relationship manager on behalf of international corporations and governments.

In recent years, Peter’s focus switched to developing economic and social models based on the ‘Aid to Trade’ principle as a viable alternative for traditional humanitarian relief and development aid.
